About Me

My name is Nik and I am an aspiring web developer. From an early age I was interested in technology, and when computers came along, I knew that they will be a big part of my life forever. My career has taken many twists and turns, but something I've always loved is tinkering with tech and solving problems. I have finally decided to turn that passion into a career, and have been steadily working towards this. The work in this portfolio reflects my passion and determination to achieve this goal.

Being proficient with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Python, I am able to create complex and interactive web applications that focus on smart and responsive design. I want to put my skills to work in a real work environment, and contribute to the creation of challenging and unique projects.
